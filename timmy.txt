Little Timmy takes his privacy seriously — or at least, he thinks he does. His new website, tucked away behind a login prompt, is his "digital diary," as he likes to call it. When asked about the password, he smirked and said, "It's something no one would ever guess... unless they know me as well as Rusty does." Rusty, of course, being the scruffy companion in nearly every photo Timmy's uploaded since that grainy selfie with the old CRT monitor in the background — timestamped a decade and a half ago. He also once joked that all his passwords are “vintage but loyal,” and that "every good secret starts with a capital letter and ends with a number that never changes.” Curious how he clings to the past in such predictable ways...
